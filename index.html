How To Use This App

This app helps you estimate both electrical sizing and monthly energy use from the same load study.

It can help you understand:

  • The likely meter/service type needed
  • The recommended main breaker size
  • The estimated cable size
  • The expected peak demand in kW and kVA
  • The estimated monthly energy use in kWh
  • The estimated monthly electricity bill
  • The appliances contributing most to your load and bill

How It Works

LOADS

  • Add appliances from the library or create custom loads. Adjust quantity, rated power, power factor, and daily usage hours where needed. Duty cycle is generated automatically by the app based on the appliance type.
  • Select unstable supply only if the site often experiences low voltage or voltage fluctuations. This makes the app use a stricter energy estimate for cooling appliances.
  • Leave main cable run length at optional or you can enter the one-way length of the cable route you want the app to check for voltage drop.
  • Use 5% for general power, appliance, and feeder checks. Use 3% for lighting circuits. Use 4% only when a project or standard specifically requires a whole-installation voltage-drop limit.
